# Customer-Trends-Analysis
A leading retail company seeks to understand shifting customer shopping behavior across demographics, products, and online/offline channels. The goal is to identify drivers like discounts, reviews, seasons, and payment preferences to boost sales, satisfaction, and loyalty.

“How can the company leverage consumer shopping data to identify trends, improve customer engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies?”
